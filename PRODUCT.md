# Product

## Register
product

## Users
Mid-to-high elo TFT players (Diamond and below grinding to climb), competitive players optimizing every decision, streamers/content creators building and sharing comps

## Product Purpose
A real-time TFT decision intelligence tool that overlays live game coaching, tracks unit pool availability, suggests the highest EV play at every stage, and provides post-game personal performance analytics — going beyond static tier lists into dynamic, board-aware guidance

## Brand Personality
Precise, Intelligent, Indispensable

## Anti-references
Generic tier list sites, clipboard-style stat dumps with no context, tools that show data without telling you what to DO with it, anything that feels like homework instead of a coach

## Design Principles
Data earns its place (every stat shown must drive a decision), Coach not commentator (tell the user what to do not just what happened), Speed is respect (overlays must never lag the game), Invisible when idle (UI stays out of the way until it matters)

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Colorblind-safe unit and trait color system, scalable overlay text size, all overlays fully dismissible and repositionable
