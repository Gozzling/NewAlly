Privacy Policy

TFT Ally — Last updated: May 9, 2026

This policy describes how TFT Ally (“the app”) handles information when you use the desktop / Overwolf companion. It is provided for app store and developer registration requirements. Host this file at a stable public URL (for example your GitHub Pages site) and use that URL in store listings.

What we collect

  • Settings you enter — e.g. summoner name and region may be stored locally on your device (browser localStorage) to prefill forms. You can clear this from the app’s settings where available.
  • Game-related signals — when running inside Overwolf, the app may use Overwolf’s documented Game Events / game info APIs to know when a match is active and to show the overlay. We do not use this to identify you beyond what Overwolf and the game client already expose to the app.
  • Riot account lookups — if you search for a player, the app requests public Riot API data (e.g. match history) via our backend (Supabase Edge Functions). Those requests are processed server-side; your Riot API developer key is not embedded in public builds.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not require an account to use core features described in the public listing.
  • We do not intentionally read passwords, payment data, or unrelated files on your PC.

Third parties

The app relies on Riot Games (public APIs and policies), Overwolf (runtime and distribution), and optionally Supabase (hosted backend). Their respective terms and privacy policies apply to their services. Links: Riot developer policies, Overwolf privacy, Supabase privacy.

Contact

Questions about this policy: open an issue on the project repository or use the support channel listed on the app’s store page.
